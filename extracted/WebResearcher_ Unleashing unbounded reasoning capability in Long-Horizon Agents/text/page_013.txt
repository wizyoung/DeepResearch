Analysis
6.1
The Primacy of the Iterative Paradigm
To verify that the performance gains of our model stem from its core design rather than confounding
factors, we conducted a targeted ablation study. The objective was to isolate and measure the direct
impact of our Iterative Deep-Research Paradigm.
